## Conversion Notes

- fl oz conversions use: 1 mL = 0.033814 fl oz; 1 cL = 0.33814 fl oz
- lb conversions use: 1 fl oz beer ≈ 0.0625 lb (approx. water density for beer)
- Barrel conversions: 1 bbl = 31 US gallons = 3968 fl oz (1/2 bbl = 1984 fl oz)
- For UOMs not listed here, ask the user to supply the fl oz and lb values directly
